- CRC 2010 >> Conference >> Overview

China Rubber Conference 2010 (CRC 2010) and World Rubber Summit 2010 (WRS 2010) will be jointly held in Mar. 15-18 in Qingdao Shandong China. There are two parts: one is CRC 2010 held by CRIA in Mar.16 2010; the other is WRS 2010 held by IRSG in Mar.17 2010.

CRC 2010 is a great exchange event for world rubber industry authorities. It is very necessary and important to organize this conference, because global economic situation is very severe and uncertain factors are increasing. Speakers invited from Chinese government, senior experts and specialists of rubber industry will analyze the situation of China¡¯s rubber production, consumption, application, import & export of 2009, and predict China¡¯s rubber supply & demand of 2010, as well as China¡¯s rubber market development in the future. Besides, experts and scholars from both at home and abroad will also be invited to make presentations on global rubber supply & demand and price trend, and to predict NR future developing trend.

WRS is a large-scale international rubber annual meeting; more than 200 members from Malaysia, Thailand, Indonesia and Russia, will come to attend it. The theme of the conference is ¡°Differing roads to recovery: managing the challenges¡±.

It is predicted that there will be more than 800 delegates come to attend these two conferences from both at home and abroad, moreover, the related persons will also be attracted to participate. The participants can not only enjoy this great platform, but also be able to learn about the latest domestic and foreign rubber production and high-end technology.

Conference Highlights

  • CRIA makes the first cooperation with IRSG; and CRC 2010 and WRS 2010 will be held jointly.
  • Domestic and foreign rubber industry experts make the presentations and answering.
  • Specialist interview activate the atmosphere of the conference, moreover promote the interaction between specialist and other participants.
  • International Rubber Exhibition will be held during the conference. Exhibition and conference match to create a first-class communication, negotiation and trade platform.
  • Factory visit will promote trade cooperation.
